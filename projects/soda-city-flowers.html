Unified parcel tracking for ops teams across 5 carriers.

Freight teams juggled separate dashboards for every shipper, making SLA tracking slow and error-prone. Parcel Scope unifies scans, ETAs, and alerts in one workspace with a resilient ingestion pipeline.

Role: Lead engineer Timeline: 4 months Team: 3

Highlights

  • Built carrier webhooks adapter + fallback polling to normalize 200k events/day.
  • Designed responsive dashboard with configurable alerts and warehouse views.
  • Implemented audit-ready shipment history with immutable event ledgering.
  • Partnered with support to craft saved searches that cut ticket handling time by 25%.

Problem

Operations managers spent hours reconciling data across carrier portals. Lack of normalized metadata blocked them from spotting systemic delays and SLA risk.

Approach

I mapped each carrier’s webhook + polling quirks, then introduced a canonical shipment schema. The React dashboard consumes a single timeline API, while background workers reconcile late or missing scans.

Impact

Ops teams reduced “where is my package?” escalations by 38% within the first month. The ingestion pipeline now supports additional rail carriers without disrupting the UI.

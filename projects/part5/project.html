Honolulu Sustainability Initiative

Project Overview

This engagement focuses on reducing landfill waste and increasing recycling participation citywide. The team is conducting stakeholder interviews, analyzing waste data, and benchmarking against similar municipalities.

Scope of Work

  • Waste audit data analysis
  • Community engagement strategy
  • Cost-benefit analysis
  • Implementation roadmap

Timeline

Start Date: January 15, 2026

Projected Completion: May 20, 2026

Status Updates

Week 1-4: Completed stakeholder interviews.

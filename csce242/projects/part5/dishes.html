Lasagna Skillet with Ground Beef (No Raviolis)

Lazy Lasagna is a deconstructed version of the classic. It's made with ground beef, store-bought sauce, mozzarella, ricotta cheese, and whatever pasta you have on hand. It's a comforting, quick, and easy recipe that is high in protein pantry staple ingredients and no need for the oven.

Lasagna is a family favorite but the tedious layering that goes into it gets old, fast. This deconstructed version is a way to enjoy this classic dish in a fraction of the time.

Salmon is by far my favorite fish to cook. It's fancy and elegant but incredibly simple to make. It's filled with protein and fatty acids, making it not only delicious but also nutrient-dense.

Honey Garlic Lemon Pepper Chicken Thighs

Tender sticky lemony and peppery chicken thighs with a coconut aminos, lemon, garlic and honey marinade to make all your protein meal prep dreams a reality. These chicken thighs are the perfect weeknight dinner.

I know what you're thinking. Chicken thighs? Won't the higher fat content of dark meat ruin my macros? For these delicious, we're using skinless thighs, which drastically reduces the amount of fat. With 24 grams of protein and 13 grams of healthy fats, this quick dinner is one you can feel good about putting on your dinner table.
